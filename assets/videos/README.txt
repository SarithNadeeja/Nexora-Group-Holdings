Hero background video for the homepage.

Required file:
- banner.mp4

Optional poster (shown while loading or if video cannot play on a device):
- ../images/hero-poster.jpg

Video requirements for all browsers and phones:
- Format: MP4
- Video codec: H.264 (AVC), not H.265/HEVC
- Audio: none or AAC (video is muted on the site)
- Recommended max width: 1920px
- Keep file under ~8 MB when possible for faster mobile loading

Used at: /assets/videos/banner.mp4

If some PCs show a black hero, re-export banner.mp4 as H.264 in HandBrake or similar.
